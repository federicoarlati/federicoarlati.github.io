Research

Thesis: The Impact of Automation on Educational Outcomes

Investigated how exposure to automation influences individual decisions related to college enrollment and field of study. Used micro data from the ACS and commuting zone-level automation shocks based on a Bartik instrument. Employed regression analysis to examine heterogeneity across birth cohorts and gender, with a focus on long-run changes in educational investment patterns.
